What have I been up to? Over the past twelve years I have worked at the very core of Image Publications. I joined to work on their men's magazine, himself, in 1997 and worked though all the issues related with a start-up. From there I was promoted to Art Director of IMAGE, one of Irelands leading glossy women’s titles. I was involved in the initial rebrand in 1999 and a implemented a complete rebrand in 2008, bringing it to a level at which it can compete with any international magazine on the shelves today. I was also responsible for developing all new titles, giving them a separate identity while at the same time maintaining house style. Products included in this work were Health, a stand alone health magazine (very ambiguous title, wasn't it), Image Parenting, Weddings, Image Interiors and Business. I also had the opportunity to be involved in Great Irish Houses, a large format coffee table book produced in 2008. In 2007 my work was recognised by the industry when they awarded me for my efforts with a lovely glass trophy. (It makes a nice paperweight). While at Image I designed quite a few magazines freelance for other publishing houses, and one newspaper. I was also involved in several start-ups, aiding them in structuring their work, IT, budgets and scheduling as well as quality control and dealing with printers. My involvement in business development of the groups’ portfolio has given me a finely balanced understanding of how the needs of the reader can be balanced with those of the advertiser. I always work in constant consultation with the relevant editorial and ad teams, in order to assure buy-in and understanding and to achieve the highest possible level of design. Current projects include the Weekend magazine and covers of Day & Night for the Independent Newspaper group, I had previously designed their Christmas magazine Mistletoe, and ongoing advertising work for Clarins cosmetic company.
