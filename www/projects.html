As well as designing Image and its sister publications on a regular basis I have also been involved in many diverse projects. For Unislim Ireland I designed their Living Magazine. I have an ongoing relationship with Clarins Cosmetics Ireland and localise their advertising for publications nationwide. I design advertising for Kilcroney Furniture in Enniskerry. Currently I am working on a corporate identity for Goldriver Farm, who have produced their own range of organic bacon. In the early days of Mongrel magazine I was called upon to advise on systems, structures, design elements and quality control, this was a service I also provided for The Dubliner in their first days. I also designed The New York Dog and have freelanced on projects for Kiss Magazine and VIP. I have worked with various advertising departments to come up with advertorial concepts for clients ranging such as Garmin, Australia Tourist Board, Vodafone, Kellogs and many more. I enjoy designing advertorials (not something you hear many magazine designers saying - is it.) For Image itself I designed a range of inhouse magazines, Weddings, Parenting, Health, Beauty Awards and advised on contract titles for Goffs.
